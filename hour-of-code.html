Hour Of Code event

We, Team 59, conducted the « Hour of Code » on November 8, 2024, at 18:00, for children in the Gara Garayev neighborhood.
The event took place at the home of one of our team members, Yusif Mehdiyev.Nurlan began the event by giving students information about ADA university and the School of IT and Engineering using the PowerPoint slides provided by the university. He explained why ADA university is the best choice for their future higher education and provided information about our school and « Code of Hour ». Each team member interacted with the participants monitoring their progress and helped them when they struggled. The coding portion lasted about 50 minutes. We concluded the event by rewarding the participants with certificates and took pictures with them as a group. For more details you can watch video Hour Of Code
